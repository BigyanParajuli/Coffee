About Us

Welcome to The Beanery, a premier coffee shop with five locations across Nepal, including three in the Kathmandu Valley and two in Biratnagar. Our first location was established in 2020 in the bustling neighborhood of Naxal in Kathmandu, and since then we have been committed to providing the highest quality coffee and exceptional service to our customers. We take great pride in sourcing only the finest coffee beans from around the world to create our signature blends. From classic espresso drinks to pour-over coffee and cold brew, we offer a variety of brewing methods to suit all tastes. Our menu also includes a range of delicious snacks and pastries, as well as non-coffee drinks for those who prefer something different. Each of our five locations is designed to create a cozy and inviting atmosphere, perfect for catching up with friends, studying or working, or simply relaxing with a good book. Our knowledgeable and friendly staff is always on hand to help you find the perfect drink and answer any questions you may have. At The Beanery, we believe that great coffee is more than just a drink - it's a way of life. That's why we're dedicated to creating a memorable coffee experience for every customer who walks through our doors. Whether you're a seasoned coffee connoisseur or a first-time visitor, we welcome you to join us for a cup of coffee and discover the unique flavors and aromas of The Beanery.
